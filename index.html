About us

Project S.T.A.P.L.R.

Project S.T.A.P.L.R. is the Society for Technology Augmentation, Prototyping, Language, and Robotics: a student technology group built around practical invention, shared learning, and ambitious team projects.

What We Build

We work on robotics, software tools, electronics, language systems, fabrication, automation, and experimental prototypes that help members turn ideas into working systems.

How We Work

Members submit projects, form collaborator teams, track timelines, document progress, manage funding needs, and learn by building alongside other motivated creators.

Why Join

S.T.A.P.L.R. is for people who want to explore technology with purpose: design, test, fail, improve, and ship real things together.
